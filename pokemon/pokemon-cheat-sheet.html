💡 QUICK IDENTIFICATION TIPS FOR FLEA MARKETS

🔑 5-Second Card Check (5秒チェック)

  1. Bottom-right corner: No ★ symbol = No Rarity = 1st print (1996-1997) = 💰💰💰
  2. Bottom edge dot-code strip: eカード era (2001-2002) = undervalued goldmine
  3. Trainer name + の + Pokémon name: VS Series or Gym set = valuable
  4. 「ひかる」prefix: Neo Destiny Shining cards = always valuable
  5. ☆ after name: Gold Star card = jackpot (¥20K-500K+)
  6. SAR/SR/CSR/CHR/AR/IR labels: Modern rarity tiers = check price immediately
  7. Pikachu in costume/poncho: Almost always a valuable JP promo
