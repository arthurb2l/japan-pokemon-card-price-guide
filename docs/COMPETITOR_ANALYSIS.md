### High Priority (Competitive Disadvantage)
1. **Price History Charts** - Everyone has this, we don't
2. **Deck Builder** - Core feature for players
3. **Set Completion Tracking** - Visual progress

### Medium Priority (Nice to Have)
4. **CSV Import/Export** - Migration from other apps
5. **Price Alerts** - Notify on price changes
6. **Trading/Wishlist Matching** - Community feature

### Low Priority (Future)
7. **Mobile App** - Native iOS/Android
8. **Online Play** - Only myopcards has this
9. **Forum/Community** - Social features

## Our Unique Advantages

1. **Japan Focus** - Only app with JPY flea market pricing
2. **Dual TCG** - Pokemon + One Piece in one place
3. **Offline PWA** - Works without internet at markets
4. **Cheat Sheets** - Quick reference for flea markets
5. **Free Forever** - No paywalls or subscriptions
6. **Card Scanner** - OCR for quick lookups
